# **App Name**: ぷらねっと (PRA.net)

## Core Features:

- Rally Creation Form: User-facing interface to input details such as location, keywords (theme), duration, and target audience to initiate a stamp rally plan.
- AI-Powered Route & Spot Generation Tool: An AI tool that selects an optimal sequence of 5-7 local points of interest, estimates walking distances and activity times, and proposes a complete rally route based on user criteria.
- AI-Driven Content Generation Tool: An AI tool that generates contextually relevant content and storytelling elements for each rally spot, such as descriptive text, engaging quizzes, or bespoke script ideas tailored to the rally's chosen theme (e.g., mystery, VTuber, history).
- Interactive Route Editor: A management interface displaying the AI-generated route on a map, allowing organizers to preview, adjust spot order with drag-and-drop, and edit generated content.
- Spot Outreach & Contact Tool: An AI tool that assists with alliance building by generating customizable cooperation request emails and compiling public contact information for selected rally spots.
- Digital Asset Preview: Simulates the generation of unique, dynamic QR codes for each rally check-in point, and allows users to preview other digital assets required for the event.
- Rally Management & Storage: Functionality to save, load, and manage created stamp rallies, including routes, generated content, and spot information.
- Rally Discovery & Filtering: Users can browse available rallies (both paid and free options) and filter them by category, location, and duration to find suitable events.
- AI Chat Guide Tool: An AI tool that acts as a rally character, offering contextual tourist information, storytelling, and guidance to users, especially when they are lost or need assistance finding the next spot.

## Style Guidelines:

- The visual concept embraces a light and modern aesthetic, reflecting connection and exploration, with a professional yet simple design. The primary color is a clear, serene sky blue (#2680D9), prominently featured in the app's icon for clarity and technological feel. The background is a very subtle desaturated blue (#ECF5FF) that maintains brightness and cohesiveness. The accent color is an energetic and warm orange (#F7891A), derived from the positive highlights (like the flag and pin) in the app's icon, providing engaging contrast for interactive elements. While individual rallies may feature diverse thematic colors, the core UI adheres to this foundational palette.
- Headline and body text font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif chosen for its excellent readability across various text lengths and its modern, objective aesthetic that suits a technology-driven platform.
- Icons will follow a clean, consistent style, drawing inspiration from the app's logo elements like location pins, arrows, and friendly symbols, using either outline or solid fills to ensure clarity and usability.
- The layout prioritizes clarity and user-friendliness, featuring structured input forms and an intuitive management screen with a prominent interactive map. Information panels for routes and generated content will be clean, spacious, and easily digestible.
- Subtle and purposeful animations will be incorporated, including smooth transitions for map navigation, visual feedback for AI content generation (e.g., loading states), and graceful reveals for newly displayed information.
